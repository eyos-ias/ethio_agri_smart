There are several herbicides that can be used for apple orchards, depending on the specific weed species and the stage of growth. Some commonly used herbicides for apple trees include glyphosate, glufosinate-ammonium, diuron, simazine, and 2,4-D. It is important to follow the instructions provided by the manufacturer and consult with a professional or local agricultural extension service for specific recommendations based on the orchard's location and conditions. Additionally, it is essential to adhere to proper application practices and safety guidelines to minimize any potential negative impacts on the environment and surrounding ecosystems.\n
