Fertilizers /n Experts recommend fertilizing your apple tree right after you plant it, and then a second time in early summer. During the second year, experts suggest fertilizing once in early spring, and a second time in early summer. Fertilize mature apple trees once a year during the spring. Apple trees mainly prefer high nitrogen fertilizer. Composted chicken manure, blood, soybean, cottonseed, and feather are excellent organic nitrogen sources. Organic fertilizers provide the Apple trees with the nutrients they need to grow healthy Apples and grow stronger.\n
